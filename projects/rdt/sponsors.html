Why Sponsor Us?

We are a team of 50+ students from a wide variety of disciplines, from atmospheric sciences to electrical engineering. By supporting the Stony Brook Robotics Team, you are aiding in the inspiration and training of the next generation of roboticists, scientists and engineers.

If you are interested in sponsoring the Stony Brook Robotics Team, please have a look at our sponsorship packet and be sure to email us at robotics_team@stonybrook.edu
