About Me

Initiative. Responsibility. Accountability.

Hi! I'm Prangon, an incoming fourth year Computer Engineering student at Stony Brook University. I am passionate about bridging project management, software engineering, and embedded systems design to build user-focused hardware and software products.

Currently, I am an IoT Data Analyst at Enertiv, where I am empowering building owners with data-driven insights by expanding our Internet-of-Things (IoT) data collection network.

Additionally, I am an Instructor in Stony Brook University's Science and Technology Entry Program, where I teach introductory programming to underrepresented minority and low-income high school students.

Last year, I was the President and a Project Manager of the Stony Brook Robotics Team, Stony Brook's largest engineering design team, where I led a group of over 50 students across three engineering teams in competition-based engineering projects.
